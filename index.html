[ cos ] · the run

Cosine is the x

Where the point sits left-to-right. It starts at 1, dips to −1 at 180°, and returns — a smooth wave that leads sine by a quarter turn.

[ sin ] · the rise

Sine is the y

Height above or below the axis. Zero at 0°, peaks at 90°, and the very same wave as cosine — just shifted along by 90°.

[ tan ] · the slope

Tangent is sin ÷ cos

The slope of the radius. When cosine hits zero at 90° and 270° it shoots to infinity — those gaps are the dashed asymptotes.
